August

About August

August is an original character that I designed for a story that I am currently putting together.

Some Background information:

August is son to the king, who has banned the use of magic in the kingdom. Due to certain circumstances, August was given the ability to use magic and when his father found out, August was forced to wear an eye patch to prevent him from using his ability and to prevent others from finding out he had such power. However, when August removes his eye patch, he uses his magic to augment his fighting prowess.
