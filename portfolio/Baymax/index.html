About Baymax Play-Doh Claymation

“Baymax Claymation” is a project I did for my Fundamentals of Animation course. I animated this using stop motion techniques. The assignment was to make an animated count down cycle, however, I decided to take a risk and opted to make this animation and include a count down on the side. To make this animation I used a tripod, a camera, Hasbro’s Guesstures, and Hasbro’s Play-Doh.

Software Used: Adobe Photoshop | Adobe Premiere
