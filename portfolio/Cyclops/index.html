Cyclops

About Cyclops

“Cyclops” is a project that I began for fun. It was a study of shape and the juxtaposition of circular shapes and linear lines. At the end of the project, I repurposed the project to also do a color study. The design is meant to share resemblance to a cyclops. The colors I decided to choose were based on getting a good mix of highly saturated colors, and desaturated colors. The original colors I chose were red, black, and white, which are Rensselaer’s school colors. Overall, “Cyclops” was a fun exploration and I learned a lot.

Software Used: Adobe Illustrator
