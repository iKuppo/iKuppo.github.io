Saving The Environment 3 Steps At A Time

About Home - E

As part of my Introduction to Visual Communication I was to create a brand and a product to positively affect the environment. I came up with the idea of a house monitoring system that keeps track of your electricity, water, and waste. With this information, the device offers advice on how to cut back or to continue cutting back in these three areas to promote a healthier environment. The device hooks up to your phone via bluetooth. Part of the assignment was to create a logo for the brand, and to design a brochure, a print ad, and a website homepage.
