Star Wars: Sith

About Star Wars: Sith

This piece was especially fun to work on. It started out as a fan art sketch in my notebook that later evolved into a completed piece. I like to imagine that "The Force" is present in our world and that there are people who can harness it. This piece does not reflect the side I would chose, but the Sith happened to give off the ominous feeling I was going for in this piece.
