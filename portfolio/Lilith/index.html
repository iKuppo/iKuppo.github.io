Lilith

About Lilith

Lilith is an original character that I designed for a story that I am currently putting together.

Some background information:

Lilith is daughter to the queen, who embraces the use of magic. When the king found out, the queen was banished and she took Lilith with her, separating Lilith from her brother August. The two were inseparable. Lilith is capable of using magic to enhance her skill with a bow. With this power, she hopes to one day reunite her family.
