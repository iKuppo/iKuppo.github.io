About Rival Quest and its Characters

Rival Quest is a dungeon crawler game that is meant to played with multiple friends. The goal is to gather as many coins as possible. To gather coin, beat your friends to a pulp! As the character artist on this game I had a lot of fun coming up with the designs and animations based on the material given to me.

Here is a brief introduction to the characters:

Miss Q. Linity: A damsel in distress, you’ll find it difficult to look away from her prominent and large biceps. She’s trying to learn how to fight with those big muscles of hers, but she’s going to have to learn not to apologize every time she hits someone.

Vegano the Floromancer: Vegano has spent many long nights studying the dark arts of the arcane and secret practice-- botany. Vegano is a malevolent and cunning magician who plans to take over the world with the power of his vegetables. I mean, how many children just toss away their veggies willy-nilly? It’s an atrocity!

Rich the Dragon Comedian: Rich is a purple dragon who makes frequent attempts at humor, often dark. People tend to accidentally call him Rick. Quotes: Nobody ever laughs at me… at least not in the good way.”

Shifter the Black: A dark wizard with a dark purple aura, face covered by blackened hood, eyes not visible, only a blackened veil ensconced under a hood. Evidently been through “a lot” according to his speech patterns. Generally spits out daunting one-liners about the horrible things he’s seen.

Rival Quest received a lot of positive feedback from many players at RPI's annual Game Fest.
