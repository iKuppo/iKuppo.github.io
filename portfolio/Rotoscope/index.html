About Rainbow Walker

“Rainbow Walker” was a rotoscoping assignment for my Fundamentals of Animation class. I decided to opt for a more “trippy” experience rather than a plain walk cycle. I did this by mixing and matching different color compositions, and overlapping the walk cycle to create the pattern you see. An online drum kit was used to generate the beats.

Software Used: Adobe Photoshop | Hydrogen (drums)
