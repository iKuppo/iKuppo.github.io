About Serena

Serena’s earliest childhood memory was of her town set ablaze by raiders and thieves. She remembers how her parents had wrapped her in a blanket, placed her in a basket, and released her down-stream to safety. From that point on, all of Serena’s memories were of her working in a repair shop fixing cars and collecting junk. Fortunately for her, her ability to fix things up in a matter of minutes was unrivaled. She was a prodigy. She attracted all sorts of customers from different continents just to have complex systems repaired or optimized. One day, a customer walked in, but not to have anything repaired. It simply walked in, dropped a letter on the hood of a car, and vanished into thin air. Perplexed, Serena picked up the letter and read it. “We are still alive. Come find us.” – Your Parents. In the letter was a map, and a family picture taken from before the tragedy. Immediately, Serena fixed up her personal robot to accompany her on her journey, grabbed her handy wrench, closed up shop, and set out to find her parents – still in the hands of the raiders from 15 years ago.
