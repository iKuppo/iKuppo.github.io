Sky Haven

About Sky Haven

Sky Haven is a utopian civilization that is composed of several orders. The image portrays two of those orders. Each order is in charge of a certain aspect of the civilization – law, military, agriculture, and others. Sky Haven harnesses the power of these semi-mythical and alien monoliths to provide power and resources for each floating island. These structures have been tweaked to be able to create a safe livable atmosphere at high altitudes, and to generate a shield to provide protection from outside forces (flying zombies?). Travel between islands is difficult considering the harsh environment outside the bubbles, but there are walkways for the daring.
