About T.B.C X Booster Squad

T.B.C X Booster Squad is a trailer I developed to help raise awareness of a dance performance my friends in high school were giving. Over winter break, I attended one of their practices and found out that history was being made. The Trinity Breakdance Club, a club that I had been president of, was going to team up with another dance group, Booster Squad, to do a collaborative dance for the school’s pep rally. After having heard that, I knew I had to get my camera out, record Trinity history being made, and hype up the performance.
