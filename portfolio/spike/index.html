Spike

About Spike

Spike is a character from Hasbro’s My Little Pony Friendship is Magic series. Spike is not a pony but rather a purple dragon. I enjoy Spike’s design and I made this fan art based on his armor in the episode titled, “A Dog and Pony Show” and Hasbro’s toy figure.

A little bit about my process:

First I collect some reference, then I draft a couple of compositions. Once I have selected a composition, I begin to roughly draft the line art. Through multiple layers I begin adding in lines and sculpting them to my liking. Once the line art is complete, I lay in the flats, add in ambient occlusion, and finalize all the values and textures. To top things off, I flatten the layers, and with a little bit of overpainting, fix up any mistakes and make any necessary adjustments.

Originally I wasn’t going to add a background and foreground to this piece, however, once Spike was done, I went ahead and played around with the piece a bit. Displayed is the end result.
